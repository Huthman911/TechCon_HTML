History of TechCon

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration. Over the years, it has brought together some of the brightest minds in the tech industry to share insights, breakthroughs, and inspiration.

Our Mission

TechCon aims to foster a spirit of innovation, collaboration, and inclusivity in the tech industry. By providing a platform for thought leaders, startups, and industry giants, we strive to drive positive change and inspire the next generation of tech pioneers.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential figures in technology:

  • Dr. Ada Lovelace - Renowned computer scientist and advocate for women in tech.
  • Mr. Alan Turing - Pioneer in artificial intelligence and computing.
  • Ms. Grace Hopper - Visionary computer programmer and Navy rear admiral.
